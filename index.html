# third-semester MCS student
I'm studying CS at NC State University taking coursework focusing on robotics and human-computer interaction. My research work focuses on using AI/ML to control drones, specifically using multiple drones to collaboratively complete tasks such as collect crabs.

I am also a product developer at CircuitSays where I work with clients to create kits to teach circuit fundamentals. Most recently I led the development of a React-based web app to server as a companion for our physical electronics kits in order to improve user enagement and product usability.
